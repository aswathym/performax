Module 3: Stakeholder Sentiment Analysis

Assessors have provided their opinions on the assessment area by way of text input. These inputs are analyzed by a technique called 'Sentiment Analysis' utilizing advanced semantic algorithms. For each assessment area, stakeholder sentiment is classified based on its ;polarity' as either 'positive', 'negative' or 'neutral'.

  • Positive = The Assessor Group expressed positive feelings (satisfaction), regarding the current level of effectiveness in the business component/area being assessed.
  • Neutral = The Assessor Group expressed mixed feelings (neither positive nor negative) of satisfaction, regarding the current level of effectiveness in the business component/area being assessed.
  • Negative = The Assessor Group expressed negative feelings (dissatisfaction), regarding the current level of effectiveness in the business component/area being assessed.
