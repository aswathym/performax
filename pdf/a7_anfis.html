A7. Adaptive Neural Fuzzy Inference System (ANFIS)

An adaptive Neuro-fuzzy inference system or adaptive network-based fuzzy inference system (ANFIS) is a kind of artificial neural network that is based on Takagi-Sugeno fuzzy inference system. The technique was developed in the early 1990s.

The Performax360 CAIRS system utilizes the ANFIS technology in order to recommended highly rated 'Ideas For Action' based on assessors input and feedback data and additional contextual information related to the relative polarization and relevance of action items.
