A8. Glossary of Terms

360 degree assessment Online surveys where assessors are selected from multiple 'assessor groups' or stakeholders, eg., senior managers, junior staff, internal customers, external customers, suppliers, etc.

Internal Functions Internal departments or 'lines of business' that directly report to the CEO or CXO.

Internal Processes Business processes performed within each internal functions, or cross-functionality, that deliver value to the organization.

Stakeholder Groups Internal or external stakeholders of an organization, such as employees, customers, suppliers, partners, shareholders, creditors, community, competitors, regulators etc.

Predictive Analysis As add-on service offered by Performax Inc. that performs 'sentimental analysis' on unstructured data received from surveys, and deliver a 'report' to the clients summerising 'sentiment polarity'.

Assessors Individuals nominated to provide feedback to the organization on the selected assessment area (functional, process, stakeholder pr individual employee).

Challenge Mapping Wizard A tool that is used by the users to determine appropriate organizational assessments based on organizational challenges faced. This wizard allows the users to describe their top 10 challenges (strategic, operational, industry, functional, process, or enterprise-wide) in separate text fields, and then connects the key words to the back-end DATABASE of challenges and assessments. In order to 'recommend' relevant assessments. This tool makes it easier for the user to map their unique organizational challenges to specific organizational assessments, and comprises the first-step (FOCUS) of the 5-step assessment process.

Assessment Design Wizard The Assessment Design Wizard allows the selected Panel members to individually and collectively select the relevant survey questions (referred to as critical success factors in Performax360), and thus finalize the Survey Instrument.

Assessment Dashboard This is the user cockpit or control centre from which he/she can view assessments selected as relevant, assessments enables (by purchasing them), assessment reports, and also track progress of each assessment (assessors who have completed what % of the survey, and who has not even started the feedback. The assessment dashboard allows the user to send email reminders to selected assessors)

Assessment Report The assessment report is a user-friendly Online report, which summerises the results of the survey, including overall assessment scores, identified strengths, weakness, and Ideas For Action. it also provides the details of the assessment scores and feedback of each of the survey questions. The report is available for download as a PDF file, and also as a PowerPoint Presentation.
